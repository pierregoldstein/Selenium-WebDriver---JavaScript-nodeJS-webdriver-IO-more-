    })

2. Examples of sychronous and asynchronous:

Synchronous:

var result = databse.query("SELECT * FROM exampleTable");
console.log("Query has finished");
console.log("Hello world");

Output:

Query has finished
Hello world

Asynchronous:

databse.query("SELECT * FROM exampleTable", function(result){
    console.log("Query has finished");
});
console.log("Hello world");


Output:

Hello world
Query has finished
